Design History Project

First, an art movement is a type of art that was popular for a period of time. For this project, I decided to choose Abstract art for the modern art movement. Abstract art doesn’t necessarily have to represent a specific thought or image. Instead, it uses shapes, colors and forms to accomplish its effect. Abstract art can be defined in many different ways such as: paintings, sculpting, photography and design. My idea for this project was to draw the half of a face figure with many shapes in the background for a unique touch and deep meaning.

The meaning of this design portrays someone as an overthinker and someone who has many thoughts that keep on circling him in the back of his brain. For this art movement, I chose to sell my design at target. Target sells a various selection of products like home décor, clothing, electronics and accessories more importantly bags! Target is the perfect place to go when looking for a selection of items in different categories. Target also sells multiple art pieces regarding the abstract art movement. I chose to recreate a design using my favourite colours and the colours I thought would fit best.
