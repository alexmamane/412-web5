Case Study

Just do it

Documentation

This project was meant to create a SIMPLE TO DO LIST APP for teachers. This app will allow the users to create an account using a username, log in or identify ourselves in the system with our username, display the list of our individual “to do” tasks, display a list of our 10 last completed tasks, provide a simple text field to add a new task, provide a checkbox or a button to be able to mark a pending task as complete.

Challenges

The most challenging part was connecting the database. We often had spelling errors and spelled words differently like words with an (s) and sometimes without. After we realized this we were finally able to connect to the db and would display data.

Moodboard

Our inspiration

We included a color scheme, typographic/font choices, photo / illustration style, cool / fun patterns.

Outcome

Maya and I sent the app to many many of our friends to get their opinions. The feedback from my friends was generally very positive and motivating. Seeing them react to something Maya and i have created was very cool! They thought it was very useful.
