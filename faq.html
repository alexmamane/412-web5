Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

I'm located in Montreal Cote Saint Luc! I bake everything from home!

Do you deliver?

Delivery is an option for big tier cakes but all other oders must be picked up!

Are your cakes certified kosher?

Our cakes are made in a strictly Kosher environemnt! This means that the ingrediants, production, process including all machinery and/ or food service process complies with the standard levels of kashrut.

How long in advance do i need to place my order?

All small orders should be made 4-5 days in advance. For all big orders including engagements, bridal showers and other, should be placed at least 2 weeks in advance.
